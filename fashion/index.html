Did You Know ?

Clothing brands often mark up their prices significantly: Many clothing brands charge high prices for their products in order to create an image of luxury and exclusivity. However, the actual cost of producing the clothing might be much lower than the retail price.

The same clothing items are often sold in different stores under different brand names: Some clothing items are produced in large quantities and then sold to multiple retailers under different brand names, even though the items are identical.
