BEST MOVIES IN 2022

Puss in Boots: The Last Wish
Puss in Boots returns in new adventure from Shrek universe to regain his nine lives by finding the Wishing Star in the Black Forest. He joins forces with Kitty Softpaws and Perro to defeat crafty crooks. Only one life left, Puss must humble himself for his grandest quest.
M3GAN
A marvel AI doll called M3GAN is designed as a child's companion and teacher. The roboticist, Gemma, faces pressure to deliver the finished version but becomes the caretaker of her niece, Cady. She pairs the M3GAN prototype with Cady, leading to unexpected consequences.
Top Gun: Maverick
Pete "Maverick" returns to TOPGUN to train elite pilots, facing rivalries and confrontations with the past, including his former wingman's son, Bradshaw. Bitter rivalries ignite as the pilots prepare for a specialized mission requiring ultimate sacrifice.
Alice, Darling
Anna Kendrick stars in a psychological thriller about Alice, who finds herself on the brink of collapse due to her abusive boyfriend, Simon. While on vacation with friends, Alice finds herself, but Simon's inevitable vengeance tests her strength, courage, and friendships.
Babylon
In the 1920s, Hollywood, the film industry is moving on, and a group of characters, including a silent film superstar, a young starlet, a production executive, a musical sensation, and a powerhouse performer, fight to maintain their relevance in the raucous scene.
